Izuku Midoriya's Japanese Katsu Curry

Description

This is a anime-inspired recipe from our local hero-in-training Izuku Midoriya (My Hero Academia), who's favorite food is a pork cutlet bowl. However, instead of pork, it will be with chicken.

Servings: 2-4

Ingredients

Chicken Katsu
    1 lb chicken breast/thighs
    1/2 cup flour
    2 eggs
    1 teaspoon of salt
    1/2 teaspoon of pepper
Curry
    3 cups of water
    1 cup of chicken stock
    1 small onion, diced
    2-3 small potatoes, cubed
    3-4 carrots, chopped
